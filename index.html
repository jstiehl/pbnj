Who we are

Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich Design is an independent web development shop based out of Portland, Oregon. We specialize in creating websites for small business clients. Looking to expand into game development and big data projects. Our tools of the trade, currently, include HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript

Started in 2013 by a retired engineer from a well known semiconductor company in the Portland vicinity, Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich Design has hit the ground running and expects to be a major player in the Portland Web Development scene in the near future. Operations are currently handled by a single employee out of his Southeast Portland home but the company is hoping to expand significantly during the next 5 years.
